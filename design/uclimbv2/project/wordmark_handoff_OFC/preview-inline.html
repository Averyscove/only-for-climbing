06

Rules

WHEN TO USE INLINE

USE INLINE FOR

  • Feed / home top header (iOS, Android) — 16 px
  • Profile / settings nav title — 16 px
  • Onboarding hero typography — 28–36 px
  • Email pre-header line — 14 px

USE STACKED (DEFAULT) FOR

  • Share cards, posters, marketing
  • App Store screenshots, splash
  • Footer signatures on letterhead, email signatures
  • Anywhere else the brand needs presence and weight

Inline is a tactical variant — it earns its place by deferring to the chrome around it. The stacked masthead remains the brand's primary form.

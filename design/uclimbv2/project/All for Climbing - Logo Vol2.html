All for Climbing.  Vol. 2

6 NEW DIRECTIONS · APR 2026

More takes — moving past the original 4. These push different territory: type-only confidence, a chunky mascot, a route topo mark, a carabiner monogram, a hangboard glyph, and a summit/altitude treatment. Pair any of these with the Stamped Heritage and Dynamic Gibbon you already liked, or replace.

05

Alpine Block — Type-only

NO MASCOT · CONDENSED HEADLINE
DIRECTION 05
Alpine Block

Pure typography — no mark needed. The orange grade-tag does the work of an icon. Reads from across a gym wall; perfect for hoodies and tee backs. Swap V13 for any grade chip when contextual.

06

Bold Block Monkey — Mascot

CHUNKY · GEOMETRIC · LOW-FUSS
DIRECTION 06
Bold Block Monkey

Friendlier and more product-forward than the Stamped Heritage. The geometric monkey reads at app-icon scale and works well on merch. Squared corners reference rock holds.

07

Route Topo — Cartographic Mark

DOTTED LINE · BETA · COORDINATES
DIRECTION 07
Route Topo

Cartographic, guidebook-page energy. The route line itself is the icon — readable from a sticker on a chalk bag to a poster in a gym. Coordinates can swap to your home crag.

08

Carabiner Monogram — A · F · C

GEAR-FIRST · METALLIC · TIGHT MARK
DIRECTION 08
Carabiner Monogram

Most iconic single-glance mark of the set. AFC letters live inside the biner shape — works as a punch-print on a hat patch, a foil emboss on a card, or a tight app icon at any scale.

09

Hangboard Glyph — Training

TOOL-AS-MARK · WOODGRAIN · UTILITY
DIRECTION 09
Hangboard Glyph

Tool-as-mark — speaks directly to the training-obsessed user. Wood + ink palette adds a gritty, craft feel that the orange disc lacks. Best used as a hero element in print/posters; reduce to icon-scale only with the simplified version.

10

Summit Mark — Altitude

PEAK SILHOUETTE · TOPO LINES · NORTH STAR
DIRECTION 10
Summit / Altitude

Outdoor / aspirational. Less about gym bouldering, more about the broader climbing culture. Works beautifully on dusk-blue and could be the mark you reach for in apparel and event posters even if a different mark wins the app icon.

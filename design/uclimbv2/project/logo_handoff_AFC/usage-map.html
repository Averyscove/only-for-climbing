A single mountain silhouette. Three colors. A rigid lockup. Everything you need to swap "uClimb" out for the new identity, in every place it appears.

REV 01 · 27.04.26 · DESIGN HANDOFF
The mark is one closed SVG path on a 100×100 viewBox — a mountain ridge with a small lightning-bolt notch. It is always a single solid fill. Never gradient. Never outlined. Never tilted.

The accent orange #e85d2a is the icon's container color and the wordmark's trailing period — and nothing else.

05 — DON'TSDon't do these.

Six fast no-go's. The mark is rigid on purpose.

✕
Don't fill the mark with orange.

Orange is for the icon container and the wordmark dot — never the silhouette itself.

✕
Don't outline the mark.

Solid fill only. No stroke, no hollow, no outline-only treatment.

✕
No gradients, no shadows.

One flat color. Glows and inner shadows degrade at small sizes.

✕
All for Climbing.
Don't tilt or rotate the lockup.

The lockup is always level. Rotation reads as an afterthought.

✕
All for Climbing.
Don't re-set the wordmark.

Archivo Black only. If unavailable, use the supplied SVG lockup files.

✕
Don't drop on a busy photo.

Always dim photography ≥ 35% before placing the mark on top.
